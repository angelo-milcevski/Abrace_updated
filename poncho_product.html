ABRACE PONCHO

The Poncho is a special blanket that helps to distress the mind by giving a feeling of a warm hug. This product comes with a pair of straps that can be taken off or put on by a single movement. The straps make it possible to attach ABRACE Poncho on different chairs, also to bring it anywhere with yourself.

Note: You can buy the different pieces separately as well.

ABRACE PILLOW

The Hug pillow can be easily attached to the poncho but can also be used separately. It gives additional feeling of being hugged and makes you feel relaxed.

Note: You can buy the different pieces separately as well.

ABRACE BLANKET

The blankets have special weight that gives a feeling of holding a baby. This provides grounding and has a calming effect on the body.

Note: You can buy the different pieces separately as well.
