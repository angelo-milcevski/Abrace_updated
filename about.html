OUR STORY

ABRACE is an organization which is made up from Aalborg University (AAU) students. The main focus of ABRACE is to help people with mental disabilities. ABRACE has a variety of products that are currently in the test phase, but soon ready to hit the market.

The speciality of ABRACE’s products is that the materials are being made with care and precision. The product’s materials are being developed in the Aalborg Universitetshospital which guarantees safety and hygiene. Thus, ABRACE’s products are completely safe and can be used by everyone. Moreover, the products are outstanding in the market since they do not look like a piece of medical equipment which says how good it is designed.

ABRACE’s staff is available for assistance and help 24/7. If you have any questions, feel free to contact us!
